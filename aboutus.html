What we do:

Founded in 1979, JAMPORT is the oldest and
largest independent dance music marketing company
in the world. Throughout its decades of dj service,
the New York and Los Angeles based firm has played
an essential role in creating a platform for
thousands of up-and-coming and established recording
artists, helping jumpstart careers and propel pop
culture status.

July, deadfish, Me Me, and Waters are just a drop
in the bucket of what JAMPORT has achieved. Each
week we upload dozens of the latest signed tracks,
LP's and EP's that only the cream of the crop of
music tastemakers throughout the world have access
to. Key club DJ's, radio DJ's, music and popular
press reporters, BillBoard, FMQB, Rolling Stone and
XM Satellite chard reporters, music retail stores
and media outlets all play, chart, report on and
market or sell the music delivered to them via
JAMPORT

A full roster of key major and independent labels
and artists have built JAMPORT into one of the
most trusted sources to what is happening
musically on the streets. Our promotion model
starts with the delivery of music to key players
of the biz, either or both via electronic download
and physical mailers (cd's, dvd's, flashdrives, bio's
and press kits). We then follow up with each tastemaker
to make sure they're playing, charting, promoting,
or selling our priorities. Spikes in sales and revenue
can be directly attributed to the efforts of JAMPORT!
